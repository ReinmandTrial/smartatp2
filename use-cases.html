Use Cases and Solutions

SmarTap's flexible inlays enable secure, seamless NFC functionality across a wide range of industries. Whether it's contactless payments, access control, customer engagement, or event ticketing, SmarTap offers a solution tailored to your needs.

Seamless Payments

Bring secure contactless payments to wearables like rings and bracelets, allowing users to pay on the go with ease.

Access Control

Enable quick and secure access to buildings and events with SmarTap inlays integrated into various wearable forms.

Customer Engagement & Event Ticketing

Simplify event entry with SmarTap, providing a seamless and secure experience for attendees.
